# Abstract Intro Video Prompt

## Purpose
Short abstract video used as an **introduction** for:
- lectures
- technical presentations
- research talks

This video has **no semantic meaning**.
It is used only to set a calm, technical atmosphere.

---

## Prompt (Text to Video)

```
A futuristic research laboratory with soft blue and white light,
abstract holographic patterns floating in the air,
slow cinematic camera movement,
calm and intelligent atmosphere,
high-tech, minimalistic, clean design,
no text, no numbers, no symbols
```

---

## Expected Output

- Duration: 5–10 seconds
- Camera: slow pan or dolly
- Color: blue / white
- No readable objects

---

## Usage Notes

- Place this video **before** title slides
- Do not overlay equations or diagrams
- Stop the video quickly (do not loop)

---

## Known Issues

- Sometimes generates pseudo-text → discard immediately
- Overly dynamic motion → regenerate with "slower" keyword
